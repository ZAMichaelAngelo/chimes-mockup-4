Privacy Policy.

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Who We Are

Chimes Crane Hire (Pty) Ltd ("Chimes", "we", "us") is a mobile crane hire, rigging and specialised transport company based at Nasmith Avenue, Jupiter Industrial, Germiston, Gauteng, South Africa. This policy explains how we handle personal information in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

Information you give us directly

  • Quote requests: name, company, phone number, email address, and project/lift details you submit via our quote form.
  • Newsletter sign-up: your email address.
  • Direct contact: anything you share with us by phone, WhatsApp, or email.

Information collected automatically

When analytics or advertising cookies are active (see the Cookies section below), we may automatically collect: pages viewed, general device/browser information, approximate location derived from IP address, and which campaign or search term brought you to the site (UTM parameters / Google Ads click ID). We do not use this to identify you personally.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use a cookie consent banner that lets you accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice is remembered on this device until you clear your browser storage. Nothing in the "Analytics" or "Advertising" categories below runs unless you accept.

Why We Use Your Information

  • To respond to quote requests and enquiries.
  • To send fleet updates, safety notices or project news you've subscribed to (and to let you unsubscribe at any time).
  • To understand how our site is used and improve it.
  • To measure whether our advertising is effective, and attribute enquiries to the campaigns that produced them.

Legal Basis For Processing

We rely on your consent for newsletter sign-up and for analytics/advertising cookies. For quote requests and direct enquiries, processing your details is necessary to respond to the request you've made of us (legitimate interest / pre-contractual necessity).

Who We Share Information With

We do not sell personal information. Where analytics/advertising cookies are active, data is shared with Google (Google Analytics, Google Ads) acting as our processor, subject to Google's own privacy and data processing terms. We do not share your information with any other third party except where required by law.

How Long We Keep Information

Quote and enquiry details are kept only as long as needed to respond and follow up, or for as long as we have an ongoing business relationship with you. Newsletter addresses are kept until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained according to Google's standard retention settings for our account.

Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Ask us what personal information we hold about you, and request a copy.
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Ask us to delete your information, where we're not required to keep it.
  • Object to our processing of your information, or withdraw consent at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa) if you believe we've handled your information unlawfully.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at office@chimes.co.za.
